Fragrance Overview Notes Reviews

Nisean by Parfums de Marly

Nisean by Parfums de Marly is a Amber Spicy fragrance for women and men. Nisean was launched in 2016. Top notes are Pink Pepper, Grapefruit and Lime; middle notes are Saffron, Labdanum, Flowers, Olibanum, Rose and Geranium.

"Nisean is a tribute to incense resins. A luscious combination with sensual leather accords heightened by smoked & milky hints provide nuance." - note from brand.

Note Overview

Top Notes Pink pepper Lime Grapefruit
Heart Notes Rose Geranium Saffron Frankincense Amyris Gaiac wood Sandalwood Akigalawood®
Base Notes Cedarwood Patchouli Cistus Amber
